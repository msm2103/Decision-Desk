## The problem

Many RV books rely on memory and ad hoc comparison. That slows decision-making and makes post-trade learning weak.

## A repeatable framework

- Build a consistent opportunity set.
- Score signals across valuation, carry, liquidity, and catalyst quality.
- Track implementation quality and realised path vs thesis.
- Feed outcomes back into position sizing rules.

## Outcome

A systemised RV process improves consistency, transparency, and governance while keeping room for discretionary insight.
